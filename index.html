Privacy Policy – FestbuddyBot

Last updated: 07 January 2026

What data is collected

The following personal data is collected:

  • Name, age range,gender, photo, dance level (provided by the user)
  • Preferences used for the matching process
  • Match-related activity (requests, matches)
  • Consent flags (acceptance of Privacy Policy and Terms)

We do not collect sensitive personal data.

How your data is used

Your data is used solely to:

  • Perform the matching process
  • Enable communication between matched users
  • Notify users about matches
  • Improve the Service functionality

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data based on your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw your consent at any time.

Data storage and retention

Your data is stored securely and retained:

  • As long as your account is active; or
  • Until you withdraw your consent by deleting your profile using the profile deletion function within the bot.

After profile deletion, your personal data is deleted immediately.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting profile

To exercise these rights, contact us at bla@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, users may be asked to provide consent again.

Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us at: skhamzanova@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy applies to FestbuddyBot.
